About Us!

Jinglebread was founded in Olympia, WA in the year 2020.

Olympia Capitol Building

Background

Jinglebread started during the Covid-19 Pandemic. The founder, Jenna Eidenmuller, dreamed of sharing her culinary and baking talents with the world. After recieving very positive responces from friends and family, Jenna decided to found Jinglebread. She had the goal to create bread for her customers just as she would for her own family, thus creating the motto "Where everyone is treated like family."
