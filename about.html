About Us!

Jinglebread was founded in Olympia, WA in the year 2020.

Olympia Capitol Building

Background

Jinglebread started during the Spring of 2020. The founder, Jenna Eidenmuller, dreamed of sharing her culinary and baking talents with the world. After receiving very positive responses from friends and family, Jenna founded Jinglebread with the help of her business associates Jack Sparling and Nala. She had the goal to create bread for her customers just as she would for her own family, thus creating the motto "Where everyone is treated like family."
